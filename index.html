Frankenstein Go

About Frankenstein Go

A captivating puzzle game where you guide Frankenstein's monster through challenging levels to collect body parts and complete his creation.

Game Features

  • Engaging puzzle mechanics
  • Multiple challenging levels
  • Simple yet addictive gameplay
  • Perfect for all ages
  • No downloads required

How to Play

Guide Frankenstein's monster through each level by solving puzzles and collecting body parts. Use your strategic thinking to overcome obstacles and complete the mission.
